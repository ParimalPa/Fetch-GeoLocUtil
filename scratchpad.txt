Coordinate by name
http://api.openweathermap.org/geo/1.0/direct?q={city name},{state code},{country code}&limit={limit}&appid={API key}

Example 
http://api.openweathermap.org/geo/1.0/direct?q=London,US&limit=1&appid=f897a99d971b5eef57be6fafa0d83239

Coordinates by zip
http://api.openweathermap.org/geo/1.0/zip?zip={zip code},{country code}&appid={API key}

Example 
http://api.openweathermap.org/geo/1.0/zip?zip=60056,US&appid=f897a99d971b5eef57be6fafa0d83239
